ShopFlow — B2C Platform
🛒

ShopFlow is a headless e-commerce platform built for a fashion retailer that needed to handle Black Friday traffic spikes of up to 50,000 concurrent users. We replaced their Shopify setup with a custom Next.js storefront backed by Medusa.js, cutting page load time by 62% and increasing conversions by 28%.

DataPulse — Analytics Dashboard
📊

DataPulse is a real-time analytics SaaS built for 200+ SME clients. Built on React with D3.js visualisations and a Node.js/PostgreSQL backend, it processes 10 million events per day. Clients reduced manual reporting time by an average of 8 hours per week.

MediBook — Clinic Booking App
🏥

MediBook is a patient-facing mobile app for a chain of 14 private clinics. Patients can book appointments, view results, and message their doctor — all HIPAA-compliant. Reduced no-show rates by 34% in the first 6 months.

ClearPay — Payment Gateway
💳

A white-label payment gateway for a fintech startup, supporting 12 currencies and 6 payment methods. PCI DSS compliant. Processes $2M in monthly transaction volume.

FoodDash — Delivery App
🍕

A food delivery app built with Flutter covering 3 cities, with real-time driver tracking, dynamic pricing, and restaurant dashboards. 4.7 stars on the App Store with 12,000+ downloads in the first month.

WriteAssist — AI Writing Tool
🤖

An AI-powered writing assistant with custom brand voice training. Built on Next.js with OpenAI API and Supabase for user data. 3,200 paying subscribers in month two after launch.
