History

How We Got Here

2019
Founded in a San Francisco garage

Alex and James quit their jobs to fix the agency model. First client: a fintech startup that became a $50M business.

2020
Team grew to 8 — survived the pandemic

Shifted to fully remote. Launched 19 projects while the world was on lockdown. Hired Maya as design lead.

2021
Opened our first proper office & hit $2M revenue

Moved into a SoMa co-working space. Launched NexaLabs — our open-source tooling division.

2023
Expanded into AI-powered product development

Dedicated AI team formed. Shipped 12 ML-integrated products including DataPulse, now used by 200+ businesses.

2026
24 team members, 127 projects, still shipping

Named one of Fast Company's Most Innovative Companies. Currently accepting Q3 projects.
