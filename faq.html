Process

We start with a free 30-minute discovery call to understand your goals. If we're a good fit, we send a detailed proposal within 3 business days covering scope, timeline, and pricing. Once approved, we kick off with a strategy sprint in week one.

It depends on scope. A landing page or MVP takes 4–6 weeks. A full product build typically runs 3–5 months. We work in 2-week sprints and you see working software after every sprint — not just at the end.

Pricing

Projects start at $8,000 for simple landing pages and MVPs. Full product builds typically range from $25,000 to $80,000 depending on scope. Enterprise retainers start at $15,000/month. We always provide a fixed-price quote before starting.

We typically split payments into three: 30% upfront to begin, 40% at the midpoint milestone, and 30% on final delivery. For larger projects we invoice monthly. We accept bank transfer, credit card, and crypto.

Technical

Our primary stack is React/Next.js for frontends, Node.js and Python for backends, PostgreSQL and MongoDB for databases, and AWS/GCP for infrastructure. For mobile we use React Native and Flutter. We choose the right tool for each project — not just what we're comfortable with.
